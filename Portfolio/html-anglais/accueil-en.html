Details about BTS SIO SLAM

The BTS SIO SLAM is an IT training program dedicated to software development and business solutions. It prepares students to design, develop, and maintain computer applications tailored to business needs.

This two-year training offers an alternation between theoretical courses and practical projects, allowing students to acquire solid skills in the following areas:

  • Programming: Python, Java, JavaScript, etc.
  • Web development: HTML, CSS, PHP, SQL.
  • IT project management: agile methodologies, V-model.
  • IT security: systems administration and cybersecurity principles.

Through various projects, the BTS SLAM develops skills in analysis, design, and management of software solutions, thus opening numerous professional opportunities in the IT field.

Details about RE/MAX France

Founded in 1973 in the United States, RE/MAX (Real Estate Maximum) is now a leading international real estate network, present in more than 110 countries. Since its arrival in France, RE/MAX has established itself as a major player in the real estate sector, with an approach centered on innovation and performance.

RE/MAX France Values

At RE/MAX France, the goal is to transform the real estate experience through:

  • Agent expertise: a network of experienced professionals who are regularly trained to meet the specific needs of each client.
  • Technological innovation: powerful tools to facilitate transactions, optimize property visibility, and offer personalized service.
  • Collaboration: a strong corporate culture that values teamwork and partnerships.

My Role at RE/MAX France

During my work-study program at RE/MAX France, I had the opportunity to contribute to various projects as an IT project assistant, including:

  • IT support for network franchises.
  • XML feed management, enabling smooth information exchange between platforms.
  • Analysis and bug fixing on the CRM, ensuring an optimal user experience.

A Formative Experience

This experience allowed me to develop my skills in IT project management, technical problem-solving, and communication with different network stakeholders. Working in an innovative and dynamic environment like RE/MAX France also strengthened my adaptability and ability to work as part of a team.

Details about the STI2D Baccalaureate

The STI2D Baccalaureate (Science and Technology of Industry and Sustainable Development) is a training program that combines technology, engineering sciences, and sustainable development. The SIN option (Information Systems and Digital) focuses on digital technologies and information systems.

Strengths of the Program

This option trains students in:

  • Programming: introduction to languages such as C, Arduino, and Python.
  • Embedded systems: study and implementation of interconnected electronic systems.
  • Creation and management of digital projects: prototype design, development of adapted digital solutions.
  • Data communication: exploration of transmission protocols such as Bluetooth and Wi-Fi.

Projects Completed

During this training, I participated in several practical projects that allowed me to apply theoretical concepts, including:

  • Web page design: creating sites in HTML and CSS.
  • Development of interactive systems: programming and controlling devices with Arduino.
  • Solving technical problems: study and optimization of electronic systems for concrete cases.

A Springboard to the Future

This training allowed me to discover and develop a passion for digital technologies and engineering. It provided a solid foundation for my higher education in computer science and my professional projects.
